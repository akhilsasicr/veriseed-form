Build a new HTA elicitation survey here: add questions, mark which ones are seed questions with a known true value (used to score how well-calibrated each expert is), then add your experts and get their links. Everything you create belongs to your account only -- no one else can see or edit it. Seed true-values are stored securely and never shown to experts filling out the form. No PICOT support yet -- questions created here won't show a PICOT panel to experts.
